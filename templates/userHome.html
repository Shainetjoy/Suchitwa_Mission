At Suchitwa Mission, our goal is to revolutionize plastic collection in Panchayats through digital transformation. Our project entails the development of a user-friendly website with a range of features aimed at streamlining plastic collection efforts and raising awareness about plastic pollution. These features include a collection schedule dashboard, alert system, secure payment gateway, educational resources, and an admin panel for efficient management. By integrating modules for users, administrators, and monitoring, our platform facilitates seamless coordination in plastic collection activities. Join us in our mission to make a positive impact on the environment and create a cleaner, healthier future for all.
